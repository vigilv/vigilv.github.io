JOURNAL PUBLICATIONS

Polarization Based Angle Sensitive Pixels

  • Title: Polarization Based Angle Sensitive Pixels for Light Field Image Sensors with High Spatio-Angular Resolution
  • Published in IEEE Sensors
  • This paper presents a new angle sensitive pixel design for use in light field image sensors. This new pixel, referred to as the macro-pixel is comprised of seven sub-pixels and can determine light angles in the range from -45° to +45°.
  • Learn more

Track-and-Tune Sensor

  • Title: Track-and-Tune Light Field Image Sensor
  • Published in IEEE Sensors
  • This paper presents a hybrid pixel cluster made up of diffraction based Talbot pixels and metal shading based pixels for determining the local incidence angle of light.
  • Learn more
